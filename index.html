As an aviation geek, one of the things I enjoy doing is planespotting which is where you go to a nearby airport to watch planes. One website I often use while planespotting is Flight Radar 24, which shows you a map of the planes flying in your area. I use it to look for where planes are headed to or where they're coming from as well as information about their speed and altitude. Another website I often use is Live ATC, which let's you listen to the radio conversations between pilots and air traffic controllers.

Design

Now that you know a little bit about me, let's get into talking about design! I designed this web page by using the the four basic principles of design outlined in the Non-Designer's Design Book by Robin Williams. The four basic principles of design are:

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity
