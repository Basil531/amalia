The Amalian

Welcome to the Amalian. this is the landing page for the country of Amalia. Here you can learn everything about the country.

Inception

Amalia is an idea that was created at the start of the pandemic. I wanted to do a worldbuilding where the country and its culture incorporated my favourite elements from places around the world. It began with wanting to design cities that had people as the centre of their design, unlike the car-centred cities where I grew up. Then it continued to wanting to design a new time and calendar system (this is way more complicated than you'd think). Who knows maybe one day it will lead to me creating a con lang.
